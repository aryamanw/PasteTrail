Frequently asked questions

Which Mac models does this work on?

Any Mac running macOS 13 Ventura or later. That's most Macs from 2017 onward.

Does it send my data anywhere?

No. Zero network calls. Your clipboard stays on your machine. Verified with Little Snitch — you can verify it yourself.

What happens when I hit 5 clips (Free)?

The oldest clip gets deleted automatically. Upgrade to Standard for 500 clips and it stops happening.

How do I upgrade?

Buy Standard on Gumroad, get your license key, paste it in Settings. Your clips stay safe — you just get more space.

Will it capture my passwords?

No way. Paste Trail automatically ignores 1Password, Bitwarden, and Keychain. Those never make it into your history.
